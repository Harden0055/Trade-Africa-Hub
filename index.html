What is automated trading?

Automated trading refers to the use of computer algorithms to execute trading strategies in financial markets. Instead of relying on human traders to manually enter and execute trades, automated trading systems use pre-defined criteria and rules to automatically initiate and manage trades. A trading bot is an automated trading system designed to execute trades in the foreign exchange (Forex) market on behalf of a trader. These bots are created to automate trading strategies and can operate 24/7, which is particularly useful in a market like Forex, where trading occurs around the clock.
